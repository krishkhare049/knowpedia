Our Prime Minister- Mr. Narendra Modi

Our Prime Minister, Narendra Damodardas Modi is the 15th Prime Minister of India. He is the leader of Bhartiya Janata Party. He remained the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014. He was elected the the member of Parliament from Varanasi.

Narendra Modi was born on 17th September,1950 in a family of grocers in Vadnagar, Mehsa district. He attained the degree of M.A. in Political Science from the Gujarat University in Ahmedabad. His father's name was Damodardas Mulchand Modi and his mother's name is Heeraben. He gained his higher Secondary Education in Vadnagar in 1967. As a child Modi helped his father in selling tea at Vadnagar Railway Station. Modi left home at the age of 17 and joined the Ramkrishna Misson Ashram in Rajkot.

Our Prime Minister Modi is a man of strong will power. He started his journey as a tea-seller and now he become the Prime Minister. He loves his mother very much and respects her. He is a very hard working man. The other countries are also very much impressed with his work. They invite him and also want to work with him. He has given many jobs to the unemployed people of India. He encourages all the sports persons as well as soldiers.

He has almost eliminated water problem in India. Modi has started "Make in India Programme" in which he advises us to make things in India and not to use foreign products. Thus we can say that our Prime Minister, Narendra Modi will give a new form (shape) to our country. His dream of making our motherland a developed country will be successful.
